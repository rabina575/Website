Contact Page

The last page of my project leads you to a contact form which is meant to be filled for those who want to give suggestions or to have a chat. This format is a direct inspiration from the above-mentioned picture. The hyperlinks present on the left side are all working links. The address link directs you to the google map, phone no. allows you to make a direct call, email leads you to Gmail pop-up box where you can see various email addresses from which you can choose one that you want to send an email from, the facebook and instagram link leads you to the creator social media sites.

Reference: Colorlib
